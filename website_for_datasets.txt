Les classes manquantes dans le dataset par rapport aux spécialités des docteurs sont :

Rheumatology (Laura Harris)

 - [Rheumatoid Arthritis Data](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/achrafkhazri/rheumatoid-arthritis-data) - Kaggle dataset related to rheumatoid arthritis.





Gastroenterology (Jessica White)

- [Gastrointestinal Lesions in Endoscopic Images](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sbinte/endoscopy-images-gastrointestinal-lesions) - Kaggle dataset for detecting gastrointestinal lesions.
   - [GIANA: Gastrointestinal Image Analysis](https://giana.grand-challenge.org/) - A challenge platform for gastrointestinal image analysis.






Urology (Olivia Clark)

 - [Kidney Stone Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mradamdani/kidney-stone-dataset) - Kaggle dataset related to kidney stones.
   - [Prostate Cancer Detection](https://www.kaggle.com/c/prostate-cancer-grade-assessment) - Kaggle competition for prostate cancer detection.






Geriatrics (Daniel Lee)

  - [Senior Health and Well-being Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/spscientist/stroke-prediction-dataset) - A dataset for stroke prediction, often relevant in geriatric care.
   - [Aging-Related Gene Expression](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE142978) - Aging-related gene expression data from NCBI GEO.





Nephrology (Grace Hall)

 - [Kidney Disease Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/mansoordaku/ckdisease) - Kaggle dataset for chronic kidney disease prediction.
   - [Kidney Tumor Segmentation Dataset (KiTS)](https://kits19.grand-challenge.org/) - Dataset for kidney tumor segmentation.






Allergy and Immunology (Ethan King)

 - [Asthma and Allergy Research Data](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/natnew/asthma-allergy-research-dataset) - Dataset related to asthma and allergies.
   - [Immunotherapy Dataset](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Immunotherapy+Dataset) - UCI Machine Learning Repository dataset for immunotherapy response.






Internal Medicine (Chloe Scott)
 - [MIMIC-III](https://mimic.physionet.org/) - A large, freely available database comprising de-identified health-related data from patients.
   - [NHANES (National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey)](https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/nhanes/index.htm) - Comprehensive dataset used for health-related research.
